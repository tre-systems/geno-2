Geno-2 // Lattice

A geometric pulse instrument built for sharp rhythmic textures and faceted light.

Starts in D Dorian. Press H to hide or reopen this panel.

Keys

  • A..G Set root note
  • 1..7 Set diatonic mode
  • 890 Select 19/24/31-TET pentatonic
  • P Reset to C Major Pentatonic preset
  • R Generate a new sequence
  • T Randomize root + mode
  • Space Pause or resume playback
  • Click Fire a flare chord stack and bright shockwave
  • Hold + Drag Carve the field: violent warp, reseeding, and dense ripples
  • Release Drop carve to lock new root/mode and trigger impact accents
  • ,.Shift Detune ±50¢ (Shift for ±10¢)
  • / Reset detune to 0¢
  • EnterEsc Enter or exit fullscreen
  • ←→↑↓ Tempo (←/→) and master volume (↑/↓)
  • M Mute or unmute master output
  • H Toggle this help panel

Modes (1..7)

  • 1 Ionian (major)
  • 2 Dorian
  • 3 Phrygian
  • 4 Lydian
  • 5 Mixolydian
  • 6 Aeolian (natural minor)
  • 7 Locrian

Tunings (8,9,0)

  • 8 19-TET pentatonic
  • 9 24-TET pentatonic
  • 0 31-TET pentatonic
  • P C Major Pentatonic preset
